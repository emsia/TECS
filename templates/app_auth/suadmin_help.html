Adding a School

  1. From the dashboard, click the 'Schools' button on the navigation bar located at the leftmost side of the screen.
  2. Click the 'Add New School' button in the upper right corner of the screen.
  3. Fill up all the required fields.
  4. Click 'Save' when you're done, 'Reset' if you want to clear the form and start over, and 'Cancel' if you do not wish to continue adding a new school.

Adding School Administrator/s

  1. From the dahboard, click the 'Schools' button on the navigation bar located at the leftmost side of the screen.
  2. Click the view button in the same row of the school that you wish to add admin/s to.
  3. Click the '+' button at the upper right corner, just below your name and picture.
  4. Fill up all the fields.
  5. If you wish to add more than one admin, click the yellow button with the plus sign (+) at the upper-rightmost corner of the screen to add more rows. If you added more rows than intended, just click the red button with the cross sign (x) in the 'Remove' column of the excess row that you want to delete.
  6. Click 'Save' when you're done or 'Cancel' if you do not wish to continue adding new school admin/s.

Adding a Super Admin

  1. From the dashboard, click the 'Super Admins' button on the navigation bar located at the leftmost side of the screen.
  2. Click the 'Add New Super Admin' button in the upper right corner of the screen.
  3. Fill up all the fields.
  4. Click 'Submit' to finalize super admin addition or 'Cancel' if you do not wish to continue adding a super admin.
Back To Top

Editing Your Profile

  1. From anywhere in the site, point to your name at the upper right corner of the page then click 'My Profile'.
  2. Edit the fields that you want to change.
  3. Click the 'Save Changes' button at the bottom of the page.
Back To Top

Changing Your Password

  1. From anywhere in the site, point to your name at the upper right corner of the page then click 'My Profile'.
  2. Enter your old password and new password on the spaces provided.
  3. Click the 'Update Password' button at the bottom of the page to update your password or the 'Reset' button if you want to clear the fields and start all over again.
